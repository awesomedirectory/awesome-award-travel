## Award Alerts & Notifications
*Get notified when premium-cabin or hard-to-find award space opens up.*

* **[AwardClaw](https://awardclaw.com)** - Award availability alerts across major airline programs, with smart filters for premium cabins and partner space.
* [Seats.aero Pro Alerts](https://seats.aero) - Premium tier with custom alerts and historical availability data.
* [AwardTool](https://awardtool.com) - Email alerts for award seats matching saved searches.

## Award Search Engines
*Tools for finding award seat availability across airline programs.*

* [Seats.aero](https://seats.aero) - Real-time award flight search across dozens of airline programs.
* [Point.me](https://point.me) - Award search with point-transfer suggestions and booking guidance.
* [Roame.travel](https://roame.travel) - Award flight search engine with cash-equivalent valuations.
* [AwardHacker](https://awardhacker.com) - Cheapest award redemption finder for a given route.
* [AwardLogic](https://awardlogic.com) - Multi-program award search with AI route discovery.

## Points & Miles Trackers
*Manage balances, expirations, and program statuses in one place.*

* [AwardWallet](https://awardwallet.com) - The classic point-tracking dashboard across loyalty programs.
* [TravelFreely](https://travelfreely.com) - Free credit card and points tracker focused on churning.
* [MaxRewards](https://maxrewards.com) - Auto-detects best card to use for each purchase.

## Credit Card & Churning
*Tools and trackers for credit card sign-up bonuses and rotating categories.*

* [Doctor of Credit](https://www.doctorofcredit.com) - Comprehensive resource for credit card bonuses and bank account offers.
* [Frequent Miler](https://frequentmiler.com) - In-depth credit card and points strategy guides.
* [TravelFreely](https://travelfreely.com) - Track 5/24 status, sign-up bonus eligibility, and reminders.
* [CardPointers](https://cardpointers.com) - iOS app for maximizing card rewards on every purchase.

## Booking Tools & Pro Search
*Heavy-duty tools used by travel hackers and corporate bookers.*

* [ExpertFlyer](https://expertflyer.com) - Professional-grade seat maps, fare class search, and award alerts.
* [Google Flights](https://www.google.com/travel/flights) - Powerful filters and price tracking, including matrix-style search.
* [ITA Matrix](https://matrix.itasoftware.com) - The pro fare-search tool that powers Google Flights.

## Forums & Communities
*Where the community trades intel on deals, sweet spots, and strategy.*

* [FlyerTalk](https://www.flyertalk.com) - The original frequent-flyer community, organized by airline and program.
* [r/awardtravel](https://www.reddit.com/r/awardtravel) - Reddit community focused on award redemptions and points strategy.
* [r/churning](https://www.reddit.com/r/churning) - Reddit community focused on credit-card churning.
* [Milenomics](https://milenomics.com) - Long-running blog and community on points-and-miles economics.

## Blogs & News
*Daily reading for staying current on programs, devaluations, and deals.*

* [The Points Guy](https://thepointsguy.com) - Mainstream points-and-miles news and reviews.
* [One Mile at a Time](https://onemileatatime.com) - Long-running blog with deep airline reviews and program updates.
* [View From the Wing](https://viewfromthewing.com) - Industry news, miles strategy, and airline analysis.
* [Frequent Miler](https://frequentmiler.com) - Strategy-heavy posts on maximizing points.
* [Live and Let's Fly](https://liveandletsfly.com) - First-class trip reports and program analysis.

## Maps & Route Planning
*Visualize routings and great-circle distances for award booking.*

* [Great Circle Mapper](http://www.gcmap.com) - Plot great-circle routes and distances for any airport pair.
* [FlightConnections](https://www.flightconnections.com) - Visual route maps showing what airlines fly where.
* [RouteHappy](https://www.routehappy.com) - Compare flight amenities and aircraft on a given route.
